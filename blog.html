100 days campaign for ODF ustainability and Sujlam

The Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS) is organising a 100-days campaign to ensure sustainability of gains made during Phase I of Swachh Bharat Mission Grameen (SBM-G) and to promote construction of soak pits in all villages to treat greywater or wastewater from households.

The 100 days campaign that will begin on 25th August 2021 will reenergize efforts at the local level and mobilize people for collective action towards achieving sustainability of ODF outcomes and achieve construction of a million soak pits for management of greywater in rural areas across the States.





DDWS launches National Film Competition on ODF Plus

To generate massive mobilisation of citizens in rural areas and create mass awareness on ODF Plus as well as its solid and liquid waste management centric components, the Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS) under Swachh Bharat Mission Grameen Phase II has launched a National Film Competition on ODF Plus.

Swahhata Filmon ka Amrit Mahotsav that commenced on April 16, 2021 will end on June 30, 2021. The competition is a part of the bigger campaign India@75/Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav.Cash Prizes of around Rs. 35 lakhs are up for grabs by winners, with MyGoV being the implementing partner.

RAJNATH SINGH COMMENTS.



By TOI on Dec 07, 2019

Defence Minister Rajnath Singh on Saturday said 'Swachh Bharat Abhiyan' has become a people's movement in the country, which is a "big achievement" of the Modi government.
Addressing an event in Delhi Cantonment area here, Rajnath said children have become the ambassadors of 'Swachhta' (cleanliness).
The defence minister said people's participation has continuously been increasing in the 'Swachhta Abhiyan'.

HONOUR OF NARENDRA MODI.



By TOI on Sep 03, 2019

In a major recognition to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's ambitious Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, the US based Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation will confer the PM with an award for his clean India initiative which was launched during his first term at the Centre in 2014.
Minister of state in the PMO, Jitendra Singh made the announcement and said,PM Modi to receive award from Bill Melinda Gates Foundation for #SwachhBharatAbhiyaan during his visit to the United States."
